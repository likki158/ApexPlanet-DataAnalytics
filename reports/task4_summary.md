# Task 4 - Data Storytelling & Statistical Validation

## Objective
The objective of this task was to combine exploratory analysis, storytelling, and statistical validation to derive meaningful business insights from the e-commerce dataset.

## Hypothesis
Late deliveries have significantly higher delivery times compared to on-time deliveries.

## Analysis Performed
- Delivery time comparison
- Business storytelling visualization
- Statistical hypothesis testing using T-Test
- Operational insight generation

## Key Insights
- Late deliveries showed significantly higher delivery times.
- Statistical testing confirmed the difference was significant.
- Delivery performance varied across months.
- Most orders were delivered successfully.

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- SciPy

## Outcome
The analysis validated operational delivery performance patterns and demonstrated how statistical methods can support business decision-making.
